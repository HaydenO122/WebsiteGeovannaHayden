Lunch Recipes

Quick, fresh, and fueling for the rest of your day.

1. Grilled Chicken Caesar Wrap

Ingredients: 1 large flour tortilla, 1 cup grilled chicken strips, ½ cup romaine lettuce, 2 tbsp Caesar dressing, 1 tbsp parmesan cheese.

Instructions: Lay the tortilla flat and spread a thin layer of Caesar dressing in the center. Top with chopped romaine lettuce, grilled chicken, and parmesan cheese. Fold in the sides and roll tightly. Slice diagonally and serve with a side of fruit or chips.

2. Roasted Tomato Basil Soup

Ingredients: 6 large tomatoes, 4 cloves garlic, 1 cup vegetable broth, ½ cup fresh basil, ¼ cup heavy cream, olive oil.

Instructions: Halve tomatoes and roast with garlic and olive oil at 200°C for 30 minutes. Transfer roasted vegetables to a pot, add vegetable broth and basil, then simmer for 10 minutes. Blend until smooth using an immersion blender. Stir in heavy cream and serve with a grilled cheese sandwich.

3. Mediterranean Quinoa Salad

Ingredients: 1 cup cooked quinoa, ½ cup diced cucumber, ¼ cup chickpeas, ¼ cup crumbled feta, 2 tbsp lemon juice, 1 tbsp olive oil.

Instructions: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooled quinoa, cucumber, and chickpeas. In a small jar, whisk together lemon juice and olive oil with a pinch of salt. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss well. Top with crumbled feta and fresh parsley before serving.
