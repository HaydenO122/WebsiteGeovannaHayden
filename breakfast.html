Breakfast Recipes

The best way to start your morning.

1. Classic Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients: 1 ½ cups flour, 3 ½ tsp baking powder, 1 tbsp sugar, 1 ¼ cups milk, 1 egg, 3 tbsp melted butter.

Instructions: In a large bowl, sift together the dry ingredients. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter; mix until smooth. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot with syrup.

2. Sourdough Avocado Toast

Ingredients: 2 slices sourdough bread, 1 ripe avocado, ½ lemon, red pepper flakes, salt, 2 eggs.

Instructions: Toast your sourdough slices until golden brown. In a small bowl, mash the avocado with lemon juice, salt, and red pepper flakes. Spread the mixture evenly over the toast. Meanwhile, poach or fry two eggs to your liking and place one on each slice. Top with an extra pinch of pepper flakes.

3. Berry & Granola Yogurt Parfait

Ingredients: 2 cups Greek yogurt, 1 tbsp honey, ½ cup granola, 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries).

Instructions: In a small bowl, stir the honey into the yogurt until well combined. In tall glasses or bowls, layer the ingredients starting with a generous spoonful of yogurt, followed by a handful of berries, and then a sprinkle of granola. Repeat the layers until the glass is full. Top with a final drizzle of honey if desired.
