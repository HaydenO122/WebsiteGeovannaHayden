Perfect Measurements

Consistency is the key to great baking and cooking.

The Spoon and Level Method

Never scoop flour directly with your measuring cup, as it packs the flour down and gives you too much. Instead, use a spoon to fill the cup until it overflows, then level it off with the back of a knife.

Liquid vs. Dry Cups

Always use clear glass or plastic pitchers for liquids so you can check the line at eye level. Use nested metal or plastic cups for dry ingredients like sugar and grains.
