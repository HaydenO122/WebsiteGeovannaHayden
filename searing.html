Pro Searing Tips

Achieve that perfect golden-brown crust every time.

Dry the Protein

Moisture is the enemy of a good sear. Always pat your meat or fish dry with paper towels before seasoning and placing it in the pan.

Don't Crowd the Pan

If you put too many items in the pan at once, the temperature drops and the food steams instead of browning. Cook in batches if necessary!
