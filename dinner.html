Dinner Recipes

End your day with a comforting, home-cooked meal.

1. Lemon Garlic Butter Salmon

Ingredients: 2 salmon fillets, 2 tbsp butter, 3 cloves minced garlic, 1 tbsp lemon juice, salt and pepper.

Instructions: Season salmon with salt and pepper. Melt butter in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant (about 1 minute). Place salmon in the pan and sear for 5 minutes per side. Pour lemon juice over the fish and baste with the garlic butter before serving with asparagus.

2. Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients: 200g spaghetti, ¼ cup olive oil, 6 cloves garlic (sliced), ½ tsp red pepper flakes, fresh parsley.

Instructions: Boil pasta in salted water until al dente. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a pan and slowly sauté the garlic slices until golden brown. Add red pepper flakes. Toss the drained pasta into the oil with a splash of pasta water. Top with chopped parsley and serve immediately.

3. Classic Beef Stir-Fry

Ingredients: ½ lb flank steak (sliced thin), 2 cups broccoli florets, 1 bell pepper, 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tsp ginger, 1 tbsp honey.

Instructions: Whisk soy sauce, ginger, and honey in a small bowl. In a hot wok or skillet, sear the beef strips until browned and set aside. Stir-fry broccoli and peppers for 4 minutes until tender-crisp. Add the beef and sauce back into the pan, tossing for 2 minutes until the sauce thickens. Serve over white rice.
